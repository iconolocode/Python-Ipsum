==> cobra <==
Cobra is a name given to a subset of the snakes in the Elapidae family, with the exception of the false water cobra, which is not in this family.
All cobras are venomous.

==> python <==
The pythons are a family of snakes.
Pythons are nonvenomous.
Pythons can be found in Africa, Asia and Australia.

==> viper <==
Vipers are a family of snakes.
Vipers are venomous.
Vipers can be found on every continent except for Antartica.
